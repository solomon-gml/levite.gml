Simple Omelette recipe

an easy and quck dish,perfect for any meal.This is classic Omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to protection ,optionally filled with your chioce of cheese ,vegetables,or meat.

    Preparation time
  • Total:approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation:5 minutes
  • cooking:5 minutes

Ingredient

  • 2-3 large egg
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or all
  • optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

instructions

  1. beat the eggs:in a bowl the eggs with a picth of salt and pepper untill they are well mixed.you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a flottle texture.
  2. heat the pan:place a non-task frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. cook the Omelette:once the butter is melt and bubling,pour in the eggs ,tite the pan to ensure the eggs heavily got the surface.
  4. add filling(optional):when the eggs begins to set atthe edges but still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillingover one of the one house of the Omelette.
  5. fold and saveas the Omelette comtinue to cook carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling.let it cook for another minutes,then slideit into the plate.
  6. enjoy:sreve with additional salt or pepper if needed
